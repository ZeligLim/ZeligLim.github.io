About

Hi, I’m Lim Zhi Lit, a passionate Software and Electronics Engineering student at Atlantic Technological University. I’m driven by curiosity and creativity — from building responsive websites and programming microcontrollers to composing music and connecting cultures.

I’m currently seeking opportunities to grow through hands-on experience — from tech internships to collaborative side projects. Let’s build something impactful together.
